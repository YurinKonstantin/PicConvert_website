Privacy Policy for PicConvert

Effective Date: June 27, 2026

We highly value your data privacy. The PicConvert: Offline Batch Image Converter & Resizer application is built as a fully autonomous, on-premise desktop utility. Data security is baked directly into the application's architecture by design.

1. Data Collection and Transmission

The application does not collect, store, log, or transmit any personal, technical, or behavioral user information. PicConvert operates entirely in offline mode, containing zero built-in telemetry modules, zero remote tracking scripts, and zero network requests. All image files you import are processed strictly within your local computer's memory hardware.

2. Image Processing and Metadata Stripping

All operations including format rendering, photo resizing, digital text watermarking, ZIP archiving, and EXIF/GPS metadata stripping are performed locally on your personal device. Neither source files nor converted results are ever exposed or transmitted to third parties or the application developer.

3. In-App Purchases

All financial transactions regarding the purchase of the PRO Lifetime License are processed, secured, and handled exclusively by Microsoft Corporation's official gateways via the built-in Windows.Services.Store API. The developer has no access to your billing details, credit card numbers, or bank account credentials.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy statement from time to time to reflect new offline enhancements. The latest version will always be published directly on this page.
